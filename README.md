## Overview  
This project analyzes an e-commerce sales dataset to identify **revenue drivers, profit efficiency, discount impact, and customer behavior**.  
SQL is used for metrics and ranking logic, while Power BI presents a one-page executive dashboard.

---

## Dataset  
Order Date, Category, Sub-Category, Product, Quantity, Gross Price, Net Price, Discount %, Discount Amount, Profit, Region, City, Payment Mode.

---

## Key Analysis  
- Revenue, Profit & Margin by Category/Sub-Category  
- Top Cities by Profit  
- Revenue Trend (excluding incomplete months)  
- Payment Mode Revenue Share  
- Discount Impact by Category  
- Price Segment Profitability  
- Repeat vs One-Time Customers  

---

## Dashboard  
**KPIs:** Revenue | Profit | Units | Margin %  
**Visuals:** Trend, Category Margin, Region Revenue, Top Cities, Payment Mode Donut, Discount Impact  

---

## Tools  
- SQL (CTEs, Window Functions, Ranking)  
- Power BI (DAX, Filters, Executive Design)

---

This dashboard focuses on **accuracy, clarity, and business insight** over decoration.
